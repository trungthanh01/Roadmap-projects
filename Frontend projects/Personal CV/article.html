The System and Box in our life

Have you ever thought about the system and box in our life?
I have thought about it.
I mean the system is the social system, the system of the world.
The box contains our mindset and freedom.
The system is the rules, the laws, the way of life.
The box is the way we think, the way we see the world.
One of the most common examples is the education system. We have been put in the system that makes us do things we don't want.
We have to study things we don't like. That is the system.
That causes our mindset to be limited. We cannot imagine the world outside the box. Then we get stuck in the trends of society and the demands of people.
I am stopping it and I am trying to break the old system and the box that ruin my life.
Now I am doing the things that I like. I am learning the things that I want.
I am trying to break the box that limits my mind.
Then one day I will understand what Freedom means. I know that many people out there are doing the same thing.
